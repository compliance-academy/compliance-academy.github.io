Having spent 18 years at the helm of the leading professional body for compliance officers in South Africa, Julie Methven now extends her expertise and invaluable insights to all organisations, through The Compliance Academy.

The Compliance Academy is my vision to meet the critical need to take compliance principles beyond the compliance officer and into a future where principle based compliance is entrenched into the culture of every business, large or small, irrespective of industry.

This will be achieved through various training and continuous professional development interventions for specific legislation, specific industries, basic compliance and specialist training.

The strategic goal of the Compliance Academy is to offer value for money by providing first class content delivered by industry experts at a reasonable price.
