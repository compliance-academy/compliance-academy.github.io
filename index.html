Having spent 18 years at the helm of the leading professional body for compliance officers in South Africa, Julie Methven now extends her expertise and invaluable insights to all organisations, through The Compliance Academy.

The Compliance Academy is my vision to meet the critical need to take compliance principles beyond the compliance officer and into a future where principle based compliance is entrenched into the culture of every business, large or small, irrespective of industry.More importantly, that business looks towards conformity with international standards and best practice. Compliance is about doing what you are told by way of legislation and regulations. We need to raise the bar.

This will be achieved through training courses and continuous professional development interventions for specific international standards as well as basic compliance.

One of the main strategic goals of the Compliance Academy is to offer value for money by providing first class content delivered by industry experts at a reasonable price.
