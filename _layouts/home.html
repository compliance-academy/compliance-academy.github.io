ABOUT US

Governance • Risk • Compliance • Ethics — Thinking Differently

At The Compliance Academy (TCA) we believe that true compliance goes beyond rules and regulations — it is a way of thinking, leading, and operating. We help organisations embed governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) as strategic enablers of ethical performance, sustainable growth, and stakeholder trust.

Founded and led by Julie Methven, a recognised authority on governance and compliance across Africa, TCA bridges the gap between international best practice and local relevance. Our work is grounded in King IV, ISO 37000 (Governance of Organizations), and ISO 37301 (Compliance Management Systems) — but our philosophy is simple: compliance is not about ticking boxes, it’s about doing what’s right, consistently and transparently.

We support boards, executives, and compliance professionals to build systems, cultures, and leadership behaviours that drive principled performance. Whether through board evaluations, governance diagnostics, compliance framework design, or tailored director training, we partner with clients to strengthen ethical leadership and accountability across every level of the organisation.

At The Compliance Academy, we are shaping a future where governance and compliance are not obstacles to progress, but catalysts for trust, innovation, and long-term value creation.

One of The Compliance Academy’s key strategic goals is to deliver exceptional value by providing first-class governance, risk, and compliance content — developed and presented by industry experts — at an accessible and competitive price.

COMPLIANCE

Foundation Courses

Start your learning journey with one of our Foundation Courses.

These introductory programmes are designed to help participants understand the core principles, frameworks, and methodologies of each management system. You’ll gain a clear grasp of the essential concepts and the role of compliance within your organisation.

After completing a Foundation Course, you may sit for the PECB Certificate Holder – Foundation Exam. Successful candidates earn a Foundation Certificate, demonstrating a solid understanding of the fundamental elements, requirements, and structure of the relevant ISO management system.


Manager / Lead Manager / Lead Implementer Courses

Advance your expertise with our Manager, Lead Manager, or Lead Implementer Courses.

These advanced programmes equip participants with the knowledge and tools to establish, implement, manage, and continuously improve a management system within their organisation.

The training provides an in-depth understanding of ISO standards, best practices, and practical implementation approaches. Upon completion, candidates may write the PECB Certified ISO Manager/Lead Manager/Lead Implementer Exam.

A successful pass qualifies you for the internationally recognised Lead Implementer credential, validating your professional competence to design, implement, and sustain management systems in accordance with ISO requirements.


BOARD GOVERNANCE

Strengthening Governance, Leadership and Accountability

At The Compliance Academy, we help boards and executive teams build organisations that lead with integrity, purpose, and accountability. Our governance services are designed to move boards beyond compliance — toward ethical leadership, strategic oversight, and sustainable performance.

Drawing on internationally recognised frameworks such as King IV, ISO 37000 (Governance of Organisations), and the IFC Corporate Governance Methodology, we provide practical, evidence-based solutions that strengthen governance effectiveness at every level.


Board Evaluations & Governance Diagnostics

We conduct independent, evidence-based assessments of board and committee effectiveness. Our evaluations assess structure, performance, culture, and behaviours — identifying strengths, development needs, and opportunities for greater strategic impact.


Governance Frameworks & Policies

We design and implement governance frameworks, board charters, and policy suites aligned with best practice and organisational strategy. Our goal is to ensure clarity of roles, accountability, and ethical decision-making across all levels of governance.


Board Development & Training

We provide tailored training for directors and executives on topics such as King IV, fiduciary duties, governance of ethics, risk oversight, and compliance culture. Programmes are practical, context-driven, and designed to build governance capacity and confidence.


Governance Maturity Reviews

Using benchmarked assessment tools, we evaluate the organisation’s governance maturity and recommend actionable steps for continuous improvement — ensuring that governance systems evolve with organisational growth and emerging global standards.


Compliance and Ethics Advisory

We support organisations in embedding principle-based compliance, building ethics frameworks, and developing codes of conduct that translate values into everyday practice.


Our Approach

Our governance advisory approach integrates independence, insight, and impact — helping boards translate governance ideals into measurable performance. We combine global expertise with deep local understanding to ensure that governance solutions are both internationally aligned and organisationally relevant.
